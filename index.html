About Los Angeles, CA

Los Angeles, CA is a large city located in southern California. Some of the city's most famous landmarks include the Hollywood sign, the Hollywood Walk of Fame, and Rodeo Drive. The city is also home to many major businesses and employers, including Warner Bros., NBCUniversal, and Disney. Los Angeles is served by several major highways, including Interstate 10 and Highway 101. The city is divided into several neighborhoods, including Hollywood, West Hollywood, and Beverly Hills.
